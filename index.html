About Me

About me

My experience in coding started in Campus where I took some units such as: MySQL and HTML

This was followed some years later by an Android Development course that I took with Andela who had partnered with Google Africa Developer Scholarships and Pluralsight. Here I learnt Android development with Kotlin and Java, database with SQLite, Firebase, navigation components and android architecture components such as view_model. Some of my Android Projects include Tic-Tac-Toe, Android Trivia, Guess the Word, Dice Roller, MyMemory game, and Swahili app among others

Later on I ventured into game development in Unity courtesy of Maliyo Game Development Program. Here I developed games such as Planeprogramming, Fetch, Balloons and Bombs, Soccer, RollerSplit, Pac-man, Driving Simulator, Match-3D, Helix-Jump-3D, Subway Surfers, Pong, Paranoid and a collaborative project: Street Field among others.

I have also ventured into Augmented Reality in Unity using Vuforia. I have developed an AR Business Card

I joined the ALX Africa Bootcamp in 2022 where I learnt additional programming language including Python, C, and Javascript. I also learnt MYSQL, Flask framework, computer networking, docker. Some projects done during this project include Airbnb clone.
